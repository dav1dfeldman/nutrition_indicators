The Ethiopia folder includes the following data: 
eth_nutrition.csv - Detailed macro and micro-nutrient intake. 
eth_nutrition_codebook.csv - The list of nutrition variables, including codes, labels, and units.
A unique household id can be build based on cq12, cq13, cq14, cq15, cq16, cq17, cq18.

The data is collected from the 2010/11 Ethiopian Households Consumption and Expenditure Survey (HCE). The HCE covers all rural and urban parts of the country except the non-sedentary population of three zones of Afar and six zones of Somali -- 28,000 households representing more than 76 million Ethiopians.
